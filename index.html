    Hello, I’m Abhirup Arindam, a dedicated and detail-oriented Data Analyst with a passion for turning complex data into actionable insights. With over a year of experience in data science and business analysis, I specialize in statistical analysis, data modeling, and machine learning.

    I hold an Integrated B.Tech and M.Tech degree in Computer Science and Engineering, and I’m currently pursuing a Master’s degree in Data Science from Gautam Buddha University, Greater Noida, IN. My academic background has equipped me with a strong foundation in various programming languages, including Python, R, SQL, and JavaScript, and proficiency in BI visualization tools such as PowerBI and MS Excel.

    Throughout my career, I have honed my skills in extracting, cleaning, and interpreting complex datasets to enhance business performance. I am adept at using advanced analytical techniques and tools to provide deep insights and support data-driven decision-making. My technical expertise extends to areas like machine learning, Docker, and REST APIs, ensuring I can tackle a wide array of data challenges.

    I am passionate about continuous learning and have pursued various certifications to keep my skills sharp and relevant. I am committed to applying my knowledge and analytical abilities to drive strategic initiatives and improve operational efficiency in any dynamic work environment.

    Whether working independently or collaborating with a team, I bring a problem-solving mindset and a dedication to excellence. I am enthusiastic about leveraging data to make impactful decisions and am always ready to embrace new challenges in the ever-evolving field of data analytics.
